About The Company

Pacific Business Park by Pacific India

Pacific Business Park is a landmark commercial development by Pacific India, an independently owned and operated group with 30 years of excellence. Pacific India consistently contributes to various industries, such as Real Estate, Education, Shopping Centres, and Hospitality. With its quality, commitment and inbuilt trust, the group has created a great reputation and lasting client relationships.

Since its inception, Pacific India has executed over 50 multi-crore projects, powered by a world-class team of professionals and an organizational culture that believes in fostering a healthy and productive work environment. In the real estate sector, the group has established a strong foothold.

Besides property, Pacific India has also taken substantial strides into education with its very well-recognized schools like Delhi Public School Indirapuram, Delhi Public School RajNagar Extension, and Pacific World School, indicating its aim to nurture coming generations. Its Pacific-named shopping malls at Delhi NCR and Dehradun have also achieved substantial success, further reinforcing its market reputation and credibility.
