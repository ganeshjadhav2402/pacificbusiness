Schindler India Expands with 2,500 Sq. Ft. at Pacific Business Park

Schindler India, one of the leading providers of escalators and elevators, has expanded its presence at Pacific Business Park by taking an additional 2500 sq. ft. of office space on lease. With this addition, Schindler now has a total of 6,960 sq. ft., including 2,500 sq. ft. on the ground floor occupied recently and 4,460 sq. ft that it already leased on the fourth floor.

This strategic expansion shows Schindler India's commitment to operational growth while helping Pacific Business Park as a thriving business hub.

Pacific Business Park: A Strategic Location for Business Growth

Pacific Business Park is a prime business destination in the Sahibabad Industrial Area, Ghaziabad. Strategically placed within a 1 km radius of Kaushambi Metro Station and Anand Vihar ISBT, it allows hassle-free connectivity through metro, bus, train, and RRTS. Besides, the newly opened Hindon Airport for commercial flights also increases the accessibility for businesses in the region.

For an international brand such as Schindler India, convenience in connectivity is central to facilitating convenient business operations. Proximity to major transport interchanges guarantees unrestricted mobility of people, customers, and resources, hence enhancing productivity and operational efficacy.
